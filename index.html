    About Me

    I am a graduate Student at Iowa State University in the Department of Statistics, and my major professor is Dr. Heike Hofmann. I received my B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Chemistry at Indiana University Bloomington.

    My Academic Research Interest:
    Business Analytics, Big Data, Word-of-mouth, Text Analytics, Topic Modeling, Natural Language Process(NLP), Data Visualization, Web Scraping, Record Linkage, Statistical Learning, Bayesian Statistics

    My current working projects and papers focus on scraping online text data and text analytics techniques for its evaluation. When I can, I enjoy developing RShiny and Andriod application as a hobby.

I served as an unit supply specialist, translator, and brigade Sr. KATUSA under the 2nd US Infantry Division stationed a Camp Hovey, Korea. It was a "Second to None" experience.
